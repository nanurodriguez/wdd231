NP Membership Benefits

Free membership for registered nonprofits. Benefits include: community listing, invitation to nonprofit networking events, access to resources and volunteer connections.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze benefits include: business directory listing, monthly newsletter mention, 5% event discounts, and access to member-only resources.

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver benefits include: all Bronze benefits plus training workshops, 10% event discounts, social media spotlights, and priority event listing.

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold benefits include: all Silver benefits plus homepage spotlight feature, premium advertising positions, VIP event access, and dedicated promotional support.
